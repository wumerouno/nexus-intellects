Why it matters

The world your teenager is growing into doesn’t exist yet.

Artificial intelligence is already reshaping how people learn, work, and earn — faster than any school curriculum can keep up.

The teenagers who will thrive aren’t the ones who memorise the most. They’re the ones who can use AI wisely, make sense of data, and turn ideas into something real.

NextPrep exists to give your teen exactly that head start — while their peers are still only scrolling.

What they’ll learn

Four weeks. Real skills. A project they’ll be proud of.

No passive videos, no theory for its own sake. Your teen builds from day one, across three areas that matter.

1

Use AI like a professional

Prompt engineering, using AI as a genuine thinking and productivity partner, spotting when it’s wrong — and using it responsibly.

2

Make sense of data

Reading and reasoning about data, cleaning and analysing it in real spreadsheets, building a live dashboard, and a friendly first taste of Python.

3

Think & present like it’s the job

Framing a problem, working to a brief, and pitching a solution with confidence.

By Demo Day, your teen will have built something real.

A data-driven analysis with a live dashboard, or an AI-assisted solution to a real-world problem — presented on stage. It goes straight into their portfolio, their school and scholarship applications, and their CV.

FAQ

Questions parents ask

What ages is this for?
Ages 13–17. If your child is 9–12, they belong in our Juniors “Future Explorers” stream — just message us and we’ll point you there.
Does my teen need coding experience?
No — complete beginners are welcome. We start from the ground up.
What equipment do they need?
A laptop or desktop computer and a stable internet connection. A phone alone won’t be enough for the building work.
Who will be teaching?
Vetted Nexus Intellect instructors, with two adults in every session — one teaching, one supporting.
When exactly does it run?
Monday 3 August to Friday 4 September (mornings), with Demo Day on Saturday 5 September 2026.
Is it safe for my child online?
Yes. Vetted instructors, a strict two-adult rule, supervised and age-appropriate AI use, and full visibility for you — see our Safety & Trust commitments above.
How do we register and pay?
Tap any “Reserve a Seat” button to message us on WhatsApp at +234 816 691 7029 or +44 730 702 5089, or fill in our quick registration form. We’ll confirm your teen’s seat once payment is received.

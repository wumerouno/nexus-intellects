Our Origin

From Researchub to Nexus Intellect Limited

We started in 2018 as Researchub, a small team passionate about helping students and researchers produce exceptional academic work.

As our clients' needs grew, so did we. We expanded from academic support into career development, business branding, global visa documentation, and skill-building programmes — rebranding as Nexus Intellect Limited to reflect the breadth and depth of what we now deliver.

Today, we serve clients globally with a team of vetted professionals and a relentless commitment to excellence.
